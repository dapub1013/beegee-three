# BG3 Dark Urge Playthrough – Campaign Summary

## Core Theme
- **Playstyle:** Calculated evil / predatory Dark Urge
- **Approach:** Surgical violence, manipulation, and infiltration rather than chaotic slaughter
- **Tone:** The Dark Urge is remembering how to be dangerous while avoiding exposure

---

# Narrative Tone of the Run

This is not a chaotic evil playthrough.

It is a **villain origin story** where the Dark Urge:

- manipulates factions
- removes leaders quietly
- hides their worst crimes
- and slowly consolidates power.
