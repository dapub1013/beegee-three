# Route

1. `Stormshore Armoury` — Buy **The Dead Shot** from Fytz the Firecracker *(Yungnastyman)*
2. `High Hall` — Buy/steal **Broodmother's Revenge** from Koll's table *(Yungnastyman)*
3. `Camp` — Hire a cleric from Withers (level 10+), cast **Arm Thy Servant**, trade **Devotee's Mace** to Shadowheart *(Shadowheart)*
4. `Philgrave's Mansion` — Kill **Mystic Carrion** for **Staff of Cherished Necromancy** *(Shadowheart)*
5. `Devil's Fee` — Pick Helsik's Gilded Chest (DC 20) for **Mask of Soul Perception**, then open the House of Hope portal *(Lae'zel)*
6. `House of Hope` — Fight Haarlep for **Helldusk Gloves**, complete Save Hope for **Gloves of Soul Catching**, fight Raphael for **Helldusk Armor**; grab the **Orphic Hammer** before leaving *(Yungnastyman, Wonderboy)*
7. `Lower City Sewers` — Show Kith'rak Voss the Orphic Hammer for **Silver Sword of the Astral Plane** *(Lae'zel)*

---

# Lae'zel

[ ] Greatsword: [Silver Sword of the Astral Plane](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Silver_Sword_of_the_Astral_Plane)
  - `Lower City Sewers` Rewarded for showing the **Orphic Hammer** to (or killing) **Kith'rak Voss** after agreeing to meet him in the sewers within the Undercity in Act 3. `X: -70 Y: 828`

[ ] Helmet: [Mask of Soul Perception](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Mask_of_Soul_Perception)
  - `Devil's Fee` In a locked Gilded Chest (DC 20 Sleight of Hand Check) in **Helsik's** room upstairs. `X: -33 Y: 20`

# Shadowheart

  - Obtained from casting the **Arm Thy Servant** variant of Divine Intervention.
