# Notes

**Aura of Protection:** This is why Eric takes six levels of Paladin before anything else. Aura of Protection adds CHA modifier to all saving throws for Eric and every ally within 10 feet. At CHA 20, that is +5 to every saving throw in the party — the difference between failing a boss's Hold Monster and shrugging it off. This aura is the single most impactful passive in tactician mode. Do not multiclass before level 6.

**War Caster:** Eric concentrates on Bless or Spirit Guardians through most hard fights. War Caster allows him to cast a spell as a reaction when a melee hit would break concentration, rather than making a flat CON check. Bless adding +1d4 to all attack rolls and saves for three allies is worth protecting at all costs — War Caster is the insurance.

**Sacred Weapon:** Oath of Devotion's Channel Divinity option adds CHA modifier to all weapon attack rolls for 10 turns. At CHA 20 that is +5 to hit on every swing before any other bonuses. Activate this at the start of any fight where Eric is expected to be on the front line rather than casting.

**Lay on Hands:** Eric has a pool of HP equal to 5× Paladin level — 30 points total. Use it as a free action to stabilize a downed ally mid-fight or strip a disease condition after. It does not require a spell slot and does not consume a bonus action. Use it freely.

**Divine Smite:** Paladin spell slots can be burned on reaction after landing a hit. On tactician, save slots for Smite on high-priority targets rather than burning them on Smite reflexively — Eric's spell slots also power Bless, Aid, and Healing Word, which are worth more spread across a full fight than a single burst of radiant damage.

**Phalar Aluve:** The Sing/Shriek choice on this sword applies a party-wide aura. Shriek imposes -1d4 on enemy saving throws in range — stacks with Bless's +1d4 on ally rolls for an effective 2d4 swing on any contested save. Keep it on Eric even if he is using a different weapon in his main hand; the aura persists as long as it is equipped, not wielded.

**STR without Gauntlets:** Eric holds STR 18 from his Level 8 ASI through the end of the game. Bobby takes the Gauntlets of Hill Giant Strength. Sacred Weapon covers the to-hit gap; Lay on Hands and Aura of Protection are unaffected.
