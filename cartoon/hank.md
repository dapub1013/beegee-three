# Notes

**Sharpshooter synergy:** Sharpshooter's -5 attack / +10 damage penalty is offset by Archery fighting style (+2 attack), Hunter's Mark (+1d6 per hit), and Gloom Stalker's Dread Ambusher extra attack on round one. Take the penalty freely in most fights.

**First-turn burst:** On round one, Hank gets an extra attack from Dread Ambusher plus a bonus action attack from Thief's Fast Hands. With Hunter's Mark active and Sharpshooter on, a single surprised round can delete a priority target before they act.

**Titanstring Bow:** Scales with Strength modifier, not Dexterity. Use Elixirs of Hill Giant Strength (Strength 21, +5 modifier) until the Club of Hill Giant Strength arrives as a camp passive. The elixirs are cheap and available from Act 1.

**Eversight Ring priority:** If running a darkness-heavy party (e.g. a warlock with Devil's Sight, or a shadow monk), grab this in Act 2 as soon as possible. It lets Hank shoot into Darkness zones that blind enemies without penalty.

**Risky Ring tradeoff:** Grants advantage on attack rolls but imposes disadvantage on saving throws. Acceptable for a backline archer who is rarely targeted. Swap it out for fights with heavy AOE or charm/fear effects.
