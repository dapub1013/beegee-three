# Notes

**Tavern Brawler:** The entire build pivots on this feat. It adds STR modifier to unarmed attack and damage rolls on top of the normal bonus, effectively doubling the STR contribution. With STR 18 via ASI and Elixirs of Hill Giant Strength (STR 21), unarmed strikes hit well above their weight class. Unequip any main hand weapon after taking the feat — Diana fights with her hands.

**Open Hand on Flurry of Blows:** Every Flurry proc can knock prone, push 5 feet, or prevent reactions. On tactician this is critical crowd control — a prone enemy grants advantage to all melee attackers and wastes their turn standing up. Combine with Stunning Strike (spend ki to force a CON save or stun until the start of their next turn) for hard single-target lockdown.

**Two bonus actions post-respec:** At level 9 (post-respec), Thief's Fast Hands provides a second bonus action. Diana can Flurry of Blows (bonus action 1) and still use Cunning Action to Dash or Disengage (bonus action 2), or spend both on ki-powered abilities. This is what separates the respec build from the pre-respec version.

**Gloves of Soul Catching transition:** These gloves restore HP on unarmed kills and add force damage to unarmed strikes. Once equipped with Cloud Giant Strength elixirs (STR 27), drop Gloves of Dexterity and redistribute. WIS 20 also pushes ki save DC and unarmored AC (WIS + DEX) to their ceiling.

**Unarmored Movement:** By level 9 Diana has +20 feet of movement on top of base speed. She should be the most mobile character in the party — leap into a group, Flurry, knock prone, leap out.
