# Bobby, the Barbarian (5e)

Bobby is the youngest of the group and the most impetuous. Swift to anger and always ready for a fight, Bobby makes a perfect barbarian despite his small size. Although strong for his age, Bobby gets most of his strength from his thunder club and he greatly fears both appearing and being weak.

## Class Features

### Rage
4 uses per long rest. Lasts up to 1 minute.

While raging: advantage on Strength checks and Strength saving throws; +2 bonus to melee damage rolls using Strength; resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage; can't be charmed or frightened (Mindless Rage).

Bobby's rage ends early if he is knocked unconscious, or if his turn ends without him having attacked a hostile creature or taken damage since his last turn.

### Reckless Attack
When making his first attack on his turn, Bobby can choose to attack recklessly. Doing so gives him advantage on melee weapon attack rolls using Strength for that turn, but attack rolls against him have advantage until his next turn.

### Danger Sense
Bobby has advantage on Dexterity saving throws against effects he can see, such as traps and spells. This benefit does not apply if he is blinded, deafened, or incapacitated. Represents 3e's Trap Sense +2.

### Primal Path: Berserker

**Frenzy (level 3):** When Bobby rages, he can go into a frenzy. For the duration of the rage, he can make a single melee weapon attack as a bonus action on each of his turns. When the rage ends, Bobby suffers one level of exhaustion.

**Mindless Rage (level 6):** Bobby can't be charmed or frightened while raging. If he is charmed or frightened when he enters his rage, that effect is suspended for the duration.

### Extra Attack
Bobby attacks twice whenever he takes the Attack action.

### Fast Movement
Bobby's speed is increased by 10 feet while he isn't wearing heavy armor. Total speed: 40 ft.

### Feral Instinct
Bobby has advantage on Initiative rolls. Additionally, if he is surprised at the beginning of combat and isn't incapacitated, he can act normally on his first turn, provided he enters his rage before doing anything else.

## Feats

### Great Weapon Master *(Variant Human bonus feat)*
- When Bobby scores a critical hit with a melee weapon or reduces a creature to 0 hit points with one, he can make one melee weapon attack as a bonus action.
- Before making an attack with a heavy melee weapon, Bobby can choose to take –5 to the attack roll. If the attack hits, he adds +10 to the damage roll.

*Captures Bobby's 3e Power Attack and Cleave feats.*

## Thunder Club

*Weapon (greatclub), very rare (requires attunement)*

*This gnarled wooden club — bigger than you'd expect a kid his size to swing — hums faintly when Bobby wraps his hands around it. The moment it's in his grip, he feels like he could knock down a wall.*

The thunder club functions as a **+2 greatclub** (1d8 bludgeoning, heavy, two-handed). While attuned, you have advantage on Strength (Athletics) checks.

**Earth Tremor.** 3/day: As an attack (replacing one of your attacks), you slam the club into the ground, sending a shockwave outward. Each creature in a 30-foot line originating from you must succeed on a **DC 15 Dexterity saving throw** or fall prone.

*DC: 8 + Str modifier (+4) + proficiency bonus (+3) = 15.*
