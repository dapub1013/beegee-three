# Notes

**Frenzy and bonus actions:** Berserker's Frenzy lets Bobby make a bonus action attack every turn while raging, at the cost of one level of Exhaustion per rage. On tactician, fights are short and Exhaustion is manageable with Long Rests — use Frenzy freely in hard fights. The Thief dip gives a second bonus action, but Rogue bonus actions and Barbarian bonus actions are separate pools: Bobby can Frenzy attack (barbarian bonus action) and still Cunning Action dash or disengage (rogue bonus action) in the same turn.

**Great Weapon Master:** The -5 attack / +10 damage penalty is worth taking whenever Reckless Attack is active — Reckless Attack grants advantage on all attack rolls, which offsets the to-hit penalty comfortably. Against prone or restrained enemies the math strongly favors GWM on every swing. Bobby should nearly always have Reckless Attack up.

**Sneak Attack on Reckless Attack:** Rogue Sneak Attack adds 3d6 damage at level 12 (Rogue 4 = 2d6, scaled). Sneak Attack triggers on any attack with advantage when using a finesse or ranged weapon. Bobby is using a greatclub, which is neither — but Athletics Expertise and the grapple/shove loop from Rogue can still proc it on an ally-adjacent attack if the target is flanked. Primarily Bobby's damage comes from GWM + Rage, not Sneak Attack; the Rogue dip is about bonus action economy and Thief Fast Hands, not the damage proc.

**Mindless Rage:** Berserker's level 6 feature. Bobby cannot be charmed or frightened while raging. On tactician this blocks some of the most punishing debuffs in the game — Hypnotic Pattern, Fear, Dominate Person. Let Bobby charge in first; these spells bounce off him entirely.

**Club of Hill Giant Strength:** Sets STR to 19 passively from inventory. This supersedes Bobby's base STR and the second ASI until Gauntlets of Hill Giant Strength arrive in Act 3. Pick it up in the Arcane Tower early.

**The magic club:** Bobby's weapon in the cartoon is a magic club that causes earthquakes. Stillmaker from the House of Hope (can cast Hold Person, no spell slots required) is the closest BG3 equivalent — a club that applies a magical status effect. Use it for the fantasy even if a greatclub deals more damage.
