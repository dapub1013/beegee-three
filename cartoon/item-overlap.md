## Vendor Hotspots

Two vendors are especially contested:

**Entharl Danthelon (Danthelon's Dancing Axe, Act 3):** Four characters want items here — Bobby and Diana both want Horns of the Berserker, and Eric and Hank both want the Cloak of Displacement. He sells one of each, so Bobby/Diana must decide who gets the Horns, and Eric/Hank must decide on the Cloak.

**Quartermaster Talli (Last Light Inn, Act 2):** Bobby, Diana, and Eric all want the Cloak of Protection, and Bobby and Sheila both want the Yuan-Ti Scale Mail.

---

## Priority Decisions

1. **Haste Helm** — the single most contested item (4 characters). Decide early who gets it; the rest need alternatives.
2. **Ring of Protection** — 4 characters, 1 ring from Mol. Only one character gets it.
3. **Gauntlets of Hill Giant Strength** — both Bobby and Eric rely on this to dump STR and respec ability points. It is a unique item; one of them needs a different plan.
4. **Disintegrating Night Walkers** — both Hank and Sheila want this for mobility. One gets it off Nere; the other needs substitute boots.
